Why Choose Electrostatic?

Well, here's our top 3...

Avoid Cross-Contamination

Our electrostatic spraying technology prevents cross-contamination associated with wiping surfaces.

Electrostatic Wrapping

Our hospital-grade disinfectant will wrap conductive surfaces. As fully-charged droplets hit the surface they create an even spread. This allows the disinfectant to cover hidden and shadowed areas.

Eco-Friendly

Electrostatic spraying uses up to 65% less chemicals per square foot.
